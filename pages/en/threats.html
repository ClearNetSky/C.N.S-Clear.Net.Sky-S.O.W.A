Emotet

Type: Trojan / Botnet Risk Level: High First detected: 2014 · still active

Description

A multifunctional Trojan focused on stealing credentials and installing additional malware. Uses machine learning to bypass traditional protection systems.

Attack Targets

Corporate networks, financial institutions, management systems. Spreads through phishing emails and compromised websites.

Indicators of Compromise (IOC)

Sample SHA-256: 0a1b…e9f4 · Suspicious C2 domains and IPs are blocked at the DNS layer in real time. Watch for unexpected PowerShell spawned from Office macros.

Distribution Methods

Phishing campaigns, vulnerability exploitation, downloads from compromised sites

Remediation Steps

  1. Update antivirus signatures to the latest version
  2. Block the specified IOCs in your protection systems
  3. Perform a scan of all systems for signs of compromise
  4. Update software to patch known vulnerabilities
